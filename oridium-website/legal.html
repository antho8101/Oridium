Our Commitment

Oridium® is a digital currency designed to be simple, accessible, and uncompromising. We believe that anyone, regardless of background or technical knowledge, should be able to enter the world of crypto with confidence — and without friction. This is why Oridium runs directly in your browser. No installation. No configuration. No wallet extensions. Just one click, and you're part of it. Your private key stays on your device. No accounts. No data collection. No surveillance. We do not observe our users. We empower them. Oridium is built with care, in C++, from scratch — with a clear roadmap toward transparency, decentralization, and long-term resilience. We do not chase hype. We do not promise unrealistic returns. We do not obscure how Oridium works. Our goal is to provide a digital asset that is fair, clear, and aligned with the values of those who choose it. This is not just a project. It’s a promise. This is our commitment.

Private Life

We respect your privacy — by design, not by policy..

Oridium® is designed with privacy as a structural principle — not an afterthought. We do not ask for accounts, emails, or personal information. No KYC. No analytics. No behavioral tracking. Your private key is generated locally in your browser and never sent to any server. All cryptographic operations happen client-side. We store only what is essential to operate the system: your public key and mining-related data, solely for assigning valid blocks and balances. These records are never linked to personal identity, IP address, or location. We use limited cookies strictly for functional purposes — such as connecting your wallet across services (e.g. wallet.getoridium.com and getoridium.com/market). No tracking, no advertising, no external analytics are involved. In short: we do not want to know who you are — and we design Oridium accordingly.

Cookies
Oridium® uses only essential cookies required for secure and functional operation. We do not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or tracking. No third-party scripts are involved. Session cookies are used to maintain wallet connectivity between subdomains, such as wallet.getoridium.com and getoridium.com/market. These cookies do not store personal information and expire automatically.
